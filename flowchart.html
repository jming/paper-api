Decision-making

Build a flowchart that demonstrates the logic of the process that you want to represent.

  • Start the process by clicking on the "Begin!" button. You will be asked to fill in the content of the first box. This often comes in the form of a QUESTION such as "How long has the child had a fever?"
  • To add the next step, click an existing step. You will be asked to fill in the:
    • Option, which is the logic that brings you to the next step. Often times, this comes in the form of an ANSWER. For example, "Less than 7 days" or "7 days or more" would be answers to the first question.
    • Text is the text you would like to include for the next step based on the logic of the option. This could come in the form of a followup QUESTION such as "Does the child have any abnormal bleeding?" or in the form of an INSTRUCTION such as "Bring the child to the nearest community health center."
See the resources page to see some examples of flowcharts you can construct.

When you are done inputting the logic, you can press the Create Tool button to generate the booklet with the logic that you inputted. And you can print the page (either with Ctrl+P or navigating via File > Print) to print out the booklet!
